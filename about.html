The DrewBasketball Camp Story...

Drew Idanol, Founder DBC

We started our Training camp this January 2022, Our mission is to produce elite basketball players than can compete in local and international competition.

Our founder is a product of a training camp as well. He played as a varsity player in his college days. He believed in the value of fundamentals training to unleash the potential of every aspiring player.

With the vision of making Filipinos world class Basketball Athlete, He started a camp together with his friends in their Homeowners Basketball Court.

Mission

To train aspiring basketball players with the fundamentals and advance techniques in basketball.And make them one of the Elite players in the Country.

Vision

To produce High-Caliber type of Basketball player that can compete with the worlds best athlete.
